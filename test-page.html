How to Test the Extension:

  1. Make sure the Meeting Summarizer extension is installed and configured with your Gemini API key
  2. Copy one of the sample transcripts below
  3. Click the extension icon in your toolbar
  4. Paste the transcript in the "Manual Summarization" section
  5. Choose a summary style and click "Summarize Text"
